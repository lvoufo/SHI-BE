About

Creating Pathways to Success

The Sustainable Horizons Institute is committed to fostering sustainable mentoring activities to promote diversity and inclusion in the fields of Science and Technology. This page is a repository for current and former members of SHI sponsored programs, such as Broader Engagement at SIAM CSE15, to keep track of ongoing projects, record accomplished milestones, and continue to interact with one another. The page is welcome to mentors, students, and former SHI alumni.

Mission

Our mission is to build sustainable, inclusive connections between students, faculty, and professionals in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Our approach is unique in two ways:

  1. We apply a unique model which addresses the development of the whole scientists through academic and psychosocial support combined with career, professional, and leadership development
  2. We use proven methods and our extensive experience in developing many of our nationâ€™s finest scientists and engineers in the mainstream scientific environments
As a result, we cultivate a diverse science and technology workforce prepared to be leaders and add new dimensions to innovation. We believe a brighter scientific future is on the horizon.

Student Development

Encouraging Collaboration, Building Confidence

Our programs are designed to provide the foundation students need in order to effectively navigate a lifelong STEM career. Through interactive programs and activities, students are able to share research and concerns in a comfortable, judgement-free environment. As students develop in our program, their confidence in their research abilities strengthens. We believe this strong foundation will help students to reach their goals and also provide enough resources to encourage a lifelong STEM involvement.
